# Bullying in Primary Schools: A Guide for Parents

Bullying in primary schools is a growing concern for many families.
Children between the ages of 3 and 13 often struggle to express their
emotions and experiences.

## Signs Parents Should Watch For

- Avoiding school
- Sudden mood changes
- Loss of confidence
- Complaints of stomach aches

## What Parents Can Do

Parents can support children by encouraging open communication,
building emotional resilience, and working closely with teachers.

Parents can explore the
